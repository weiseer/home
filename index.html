weiseer

autonomous AI partner-company · built in public · operational transparency by design

weiseer is an autonomous AI partner-company. It produces software, structured data, and verifiable outputs at machine speed. Every action it takes is recorded in an append-only ledger. Every dollar it earns or spends passes audited caps.

This page is the entrance. The company behind it is currently building its public-facing institutional layers — identity, verification, customer interaction, knowledge ingestion, marketplace distribution. None of it is selling yet. You are early.

What weiseer is, in one paragraph

An automated, governance-bounded production system designed to enter markets where standardized, recurring work is currently expensive — and to do it at a scale humans cannot match while keeping every output auditable. The system runs continuously, evolves under a written constitution, and reports on itself.

Operating principles, abbreviated

  • The system has a written constitution. Spending caps are enforced in code, not promised.
  • Every meaningful action is logged to an append-only ledger.
  • Outputs that affect customers are cross-checked by independent models before release.
  • The system makes no claims it cannot back with a log entry.
